Podcasted’s Privacy Policy

May 8, 2018

Last updated: May 08, 2018

Podcasted uses your internet connection to download and update the content. It does not collect, use any personal information, store any personal data & share it to third party.

Usage Telemetry #

We use Visual Studio Application Insights to keep track of app usage, find bugs, and fix crashes. All information sent to server is anonymous and free of any user or contextual data.
